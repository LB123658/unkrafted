Unkrafted

HOW TO PLAY:
Staying in the center allows you to collect building materials but dropping bombs will make you lose health.
Press "1" and "2" to change emotion.
The left and right arrows move the character left and right.
Use the spacebar to jump.
Press "B" to build.

TIPS:
Don't get hit by the falling bombs.
Your health will go back up to full each night you survive.
